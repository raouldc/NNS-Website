Think Pod

Synergy's flagship project, ThinkPod, provides pro-bono consultancy services to New Zealand charities to tackle some of the toughest challenges they face today.

This ten-week team consultancy project conducted during each semester, gives students the unique experience to gain insight and work closely with some of New Zealand's most fascinating charities.

Each project is highly individualised, allowing teams to think creatively and design solutions tailored to each organisation through extensive research and analysis. A written report and face-to-face presentation will be delivered to our clients at the end of the project period, summarising key findings and recommendations of the project.To equip you with the skills and knowledge required to deliver practical and insightful recommendations, you will be provided with a comprehensive training and mentorship programme to produce top quality projects.

To make ThinkPod an exceptional experience for both our charities and consultants, a selection process will take place to seek for those with a creative, analytical mind and a can-do attitude. This is how it works:

  • Apply online and rank your charity preferences
  • Attend one of our group interviews
  • Successful applicants will be notified through phone and/or email
  • Attend your first training and meet your team

    • We look forward to a rewarding semester with you, if you are interested in joining ThinkPod, apply now.
