About ThinkPod

ThinkPod is a pro-bono consultancy service that strives to recognise your unique needs and designs creative solutions that are especially tailored to your organisation. We offer a fresh perspective in tackling some of the toughest challenges charities and other Social Change Organisations (hyperlink) face and provide pragmatic recommendations. Our consultancy topics range from marketing, human resources, finance and administration just to name a few.

How it works

Every semester we recruit and select teams of university students with a creative and analytical mind to work closely with your organisation.To ensure top quality services, Synergy will provide students with a comprehensive training program as well as mentorship from experienced individuals to support them throughout the project.
